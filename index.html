The Process
Effortless in
four steps.

We've engineered every touchpoint so your journey begins the moment you leave home.

01
Schedule your pickup
Book online or via our app. Choose a convenient time window and our agent arrives at your doorstep.
02
IoT tags applied
Each bag gets a smart IoT sensor — giving you tamper alerts, temperature logs, and live location in real time.
03
AI-optimised route
Our AI calculates the fastest, safest route to your station — adapting dynamically to traffic and rail schedules.
04
Collect at the station
Your bags wait at a secure, staffed BagEase counter at your departure station, verified by digital ID.
